BMCC Information

Borough of Manhattan Community College (BMCC) is a public college located in downtown Manhattan in New York City, and is part of the City University of New York (CUNY) system. BMCC offers over 50 associate degree programs in areas such as liberal arts, science, business, and health. With a diverse student population of over 25,000, BMCC provides a range of support services, including counseling, tutoring, and career services, to help students succeed. The college is known for its affordable tuition and commitment to accessibility.
